Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI Matching Engine work?
Our AI analyzes over 50 data points from a candidate's profile, including their verified challenge results, GitHub repositories, and past experience. It maps these against your job requirements to provide an accurate percentage match, saving you hours of manual screening.
Are the skill badges actually credible?
Yes. Projobers requires candidates to pass realistic, timed coding assessments and systemic architecture challenges to earn a 'Verified' badge. This ensures they possess the actual capability, not just keyword stuffing on their resume.
Can I integrate Projobers with my existing ATS?
Absolutely. We offer direct integrations with popular Applicant Tracking Systems like Workday, Greenhouse, and Lever. Export top matches into your existing pipeline with a single click.
What pricing plans do you offer for employers?
We offer flexible plans ranging from Pay-Per-Post for small startups, to comprehensive Enterprise subscriptions that unlock unlimited postings, full access to the AI Sourcing Assistant, and dedicated account management.
